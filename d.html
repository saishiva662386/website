About Us

Venkateswara Cement Works has been a pioneer in the cement manufacturing industry for over 25 years. With a commitment to quality and innovation, we have become one of the leading cement manufacturers in the region. Our product range is designed to meet a variety of needs for both residential and commercial projects. We proudly manufacture and offer a wide selection of cement-based products, including:
Cement Benches– Durable benches for parks, gardens, and public spaces.
Cement Doors and Windows – Strong, weather-resistant frames for various architectural styles.
Ventilators – Quality ventilators to ensure proper airflow in buildings.
Vibrating Poles– Reinforced poles suitable for structural applications.
Cement Rings – Reliable rings for water storage and septic tank use.
Flower Pots* – Stylish cement flower pots ideal for indoor and outdoor use.
Decorative Items – Intricately designed gods' images, flower décor, and other ornamental cement items for home decoration.
